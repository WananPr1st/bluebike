HOW IT WORKS

The bluegogo mobile app is all you need to get started

FIND

We're right around the corner. Check the app for bikes near you.

RESERVE

UNLOCK

You are good to go! Enjoy the ride.

LOCK

Push lever to lock the bike. And pay directly via the app.
